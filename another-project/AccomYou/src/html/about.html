About
AccomYou

Purpose of making

AcommYou is here to Accompany you with professional verified Courses ranging from basic to intermediate, our mission is to encourage young people to achieve their dreams and make them come true, with the help of our fun community and our professional teachers, everything is possible here.

Target Person

✧ medical student:

AccomYou provides medical courses so that medical students can study medical material again

✧ elementary school students - college students or even parents:

Elementary school students and college students really need more education so they can learn more about the material but there are only a few in our area.

✧ general public

Anyone can study at accomYou as long as they have the intention and purpose of learning

✧ people who want to study abroad

We also provide language lessons for people who want to learn languages, but unfortunately at the moment we only provide 5 languages, especially English, but unfortunately the English language is only Indonesian-English.
