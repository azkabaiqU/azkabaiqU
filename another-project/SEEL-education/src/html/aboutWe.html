Website yang bernama Seel yang dibuat oleh 3 anggota tim, website ini tentang edukasi pembelajaran, website ini berisi 2 page, dibuat tanpa tailwind karena ini dibuat sebelum saya belajar tailwind, atau baru pertama kali belajar tailwind, saya sebagai developer utama, membuat website ini dengan bantuan 2 anggota tim lainnya, yaitu Reveeno dan Mawaldy, website ini dibuat untuk mengikuti lomba yang diadakan oleh Universitas Negeri Malang, website ini dibuat dengan menggunakan HTML, CSS, dan JavaScript, website ini juga sudah responsive.

History

The SEEL development team comes from a variety of backgrounds, including experienced educators, technologists, and interface designers. They unite to design education from various learning materials and from genius professionals on earth Along with technological developments, SEEL offers more than just offline learning. By using easy learning methods, namely online learning, such as distance using Zoom and etc., SEEL takes education to a new level, and easily anywhere. They believe that education is the key to unlocking hidden potential, and in this spirit, they launched SEEL education.

Vision & Mission

SEEL's mission is to empower every individual to achieve personal success. This website offers courses from various disciplines, from science to unique things. SEEL's vision is to create a world where education is no longer limited by geographic, economic, or social boundaries.
